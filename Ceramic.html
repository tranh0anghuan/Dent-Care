Aesthetic Porcelain Veneers: Beautiful, Safe, and What You Need to Know

Aesthetic porcelain veneers are a fixed restoration technique using porcelain material that restores chewing function, improves aesthetics, and helps you feel confident with a naturally bright smile. Questions about whether getting porcelain veneers is painful, the types of porcelain veneers, and the cost of porcelain veneers will be answered in the following article.

What are Porcelain Veneers? When Should You Get Porcelain Veneers?

Porcelain veneers (fixed porcelain restoration) involve using porcelain or porcelain combined with metal to cover defective or damaged teeth, restoring their shape, size, and color to resemble natural teeth.

There are two methods of porcelain veneers:

  • Porcelain Crowns: This method uses a porcelain cap to cover the natural tooth.
  • Porcelain Veneers: This method uses a thin porcelain layer with the shape and size of natural teeth to cover the front surface of the tooth. This method is suitable for customers with already well-aligned teeth.

Four Advantages of Aesthetic Porcelain Veneers

Improving the Aesthetic Appearance of Teeth

Porcelain veneers are designed with a more beautiful color and shape than the original teeth, so after getting aesthetic porcelain veneers, they can significantly correct defects in bad, broken, or chipped teeth, making them look beautiful, even, and perfectly white.

Restoring Chewing Function

Porcelain veneers are very hard and durable, providing good chewing force, so after getting porcelain veneers, the natural teeth are not only protected and strengthened but also ensure good chewing function. Especially in cases of missing teeth, porcelain veneers used to create a bridge can help restore the missing teeth and improve the overall chewing ability of the entire mouth.
