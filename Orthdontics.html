What are Braces?

Braces are a method that uses specialized appliances, either fixed or removable, to help move and align teeth into the correct position. This gives customers a straight, beautiful smile, ensuring proper chewing function and bite alignment...

When Should You Get Braces?

This method is indicated and highly effective in correcting the following dental issues:

  • Protruding Teeth: Causes facial aesthetics issues, and teeth wear down more during chewing.
  • Underbite, Shovel-shaped Face: Causes facial aesthetics issues, affects chewing function, and may cause speech difficulties...
  • Spaced, Gapped Teeth: Leads to weak, easily loosened teeth, food frequently gets stuck between teeth, and affects pronunciation...
  • Crowded, Overlapping Teeth: Causes facial aesthetics issues, food gets stuck between teeth, and makes dental care difficult...
  • Open Bite, Teeth Do Not Meet: Makes chewing difficult, may cause speech difficulties, and poor pronunciation...
  • Deep Bite, Recessed Chin, Smile Does Not Show Lower Teeth: Causes facial imbalance, affects temporomandibular joint function...

Common Types of Braces

There are various orthodontic methods to meet the diverse needs of customers. Depending on the condition of your teeth, desires, and financial situation, the dentist will recommend suitable aesthetic braces.

Metal Braces

Metal braces involve fixing a system of brackets and wires onto the teeth, creating force to move the teeth into the desired position. There are several types of brackets:

  • Standard Metal Brackets: Highly effective due to their sturdy metal frame, the force exerted by the brackets and bands makes it easier to adjust teeth to the desired position. Cost: from 35,000,000 VND.
  • Ceramic Brackets: With brackets and bands matching the natural tooth color, ceramic braces offer high aesthetic value, making customers feel more confident in daily communication. Cost: from 40,000,000 VND.
  • Self-ligating Brackets: Designed with flexible clips (metal or ceramic) that can open and close to secure the wire in the bracket slot, allowing the wire to slide freely. This maintains stable force, reduces friction, and minimizes pain and the risk of bracket detachment. Cost: from 40,000,000 VND.
  • Lingual Braces: The braces are placed on the inside of the teeth, providing high aesthetic value as you can smile and communicate freely without showing any braces. However, they move teeth slowly and can be uncomfortable. Cost: around 100,000,000 VND.

    • Clear Aligners

    Instead of using a system of brackets and wires, clear aligners use transparent, flexible plastic trays that fit snugly over the teeth, creating force to move and align misaligned teeth. Cost: from 42,000,000 VND.

  • High Aesthetic Value: The aligners are nearly invisible, so you don't have to worry about anyone noticing them.
  • Easy to Clean: Since the aligners are removable, you can brush and floss your teeth more easily than with other methods.
  • Convenient Removal and Replacement: You can remove the aligners when eating or cleaning your teeth, then easily put them back in place, allowing you to enjoy life fully.
